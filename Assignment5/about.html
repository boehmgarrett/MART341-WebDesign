Reasons Behind These Websites

Fanfiction Website

This would be a great idea because people can put down any idea they want in this website. There will be limits such as the use of language, but any idea will be welcomed here. People can put down more episode ideas for a TV show that's already been canceled.

"What If" Website

This website will be a great opportunity to share some changes I would make in TV shows or movies. If there was an episode that wasn't good enough for me I would put down what I would do to make it better. This is another way to share my big ideas to others around me.

Video Games Website

This would a great way to express my feeling about certain video games. I can put down many details about video games and what the goals are. I can even type down details about video games that I would like to make on my own.
